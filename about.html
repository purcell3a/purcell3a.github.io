Who am I?

In 2020 along with many others I shut the doors to my production company and the NGO I co-founded at 19.
I've always had a deep appreciation for software solutions and was excited to find I enjoy building them!

I am deeply passionate about technology that unifies educates, and empowers.
I love making our everyday lives easier and bringing clarity and simplicity to key life decisions.

(TLDR; I like software that helps people buy their first house or get groceries!)

I'm very goal oriented and I like to play this to my advantage by breaking up processes into smallers steps.

I'm motivated by challenge and I'm happiest when surrounded by a team more knowledgable than myself.

I have a deep drive to create, when I'm not programming you'll find me traveling, planning get-togethers or making art with LED's, fabric and paint!
