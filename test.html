Who am I?

I've always had a deep appreciation for software solutions and was excited to find I enjoy building them!

I am deeply passionate about technology that unifies educates, and empowers.
I love making our everyday lives easier and bringing clarity and simplicity to key life decisions.

(TLDR; I like software that helps people buy their first house or get groceries!)
